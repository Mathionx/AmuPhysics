Physics Formula Reference
Mechanics & SHM
  • \(T = 2\pi\sqrt{\frac{m}{k}}\) (spring-mass)
  • \(T = 2\pi\sqrt{\frac{L}{g}}\) (pendulum)
  • \(\omega = 2\pi f = \frac{2\pi}{T}\)
  • \(v_{max} = \omega A\)
  • \(a_{max} = \omega^2 A\)
  • \(F = -kx\) (Hooke's law)
Electricity & Magnetism
  • \(F = k\frac{q_1q_2}{r^2}\) (Coulomb's law)
  • \(E = \frac{F}{q} = \frac{kq}{r^2}\)
  • \(V = IR\) (Ohm's law)
  • \(P = IV = I^2R = \frac{V^2}{R}\)
  • \(\mathcal{E} = -\frac{d\Phi_B}{dt}\) (Faraday's law)
  • \(\mathbf{F} = q\mathbf{v} \times \mathbf{B}\) (Lorentz force)
Thermodynamics
  • \(Q = mc\Delta T\) (specific heat)
  • \(Q = mL\) (latent heat)
  • \(\Delta U = Q - W\) (1st law)
  • Adiabatic: \(Q = 0\), \(\Delta U = -W\)
  • Isochoric: \(\Delta V = 0\), \(W = 0\)
  • Isobaric: \(\Delta P = 0\), \(W = P\Delta V\)
Waves & Properties
  • \(v = f\lambda\) (wave speed)
  • \(T = \frac{1}{f}\) (period)
  • \(Y = \frac{FL}{A\Delta L}\) (Young's modulus)
  • \(S = \frac{F/A}{\Delta x / h}\) (shear modulus)
  • \(f' = f \frac{v \pm v_o}{v \mp v_s}\) (Doppler effect)
